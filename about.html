About PromptBetter

PromptBetter is a free, open collection of curated AI prompt templates designed to help you get better results from AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Midjourney.

Our Mission

We believe everyone should be able to harness the power of AI effectively. Great prompts are the key to great AI output, and they shouldn't be locked behind paywalls.

What We Offer

  • 500+ Free Prompts: Covering writing, coding, marketing, business, education, and more
  • Tested & Optimized: Every prompt is tested across multiple AI models
  • Daily Updates: New prompts added regularly based on community feedback
  • Copy & Paste Ready: One-click copy for immediate use
  • Model-Specific Tips: Know which prompts work best with which AI

How This Site is Supported

PromptBetter is free to use. We sustain the project through:

  • Voluntary donations via Buy Me a Coffee
  • Affiliate links to AI tools (we may earn a small commission if you sign up)
  • Non-intrusive display ads
